Cookies Policy

We are committed to providing an accessible online experience for all visitors, including those with disabilities. To ensure we offer an inclusive and functional website, we may use cookies and similar tracking technologies.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit a website. They help improve your browsing experience by remembering your preferences and settings, enabling certain features, and providing insights on how you use the website.

How We Use Cookies for Accessibility

We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance the accessibility of our website. Cookies can store your accessibility settings, such as text size or contrast preferences, allowing us to remeber your preferences across sessions. We may use cookies to store session information, so you don't have to repeatedly input your preferences.

Managing and Controlling Cookies

You have the ability to control cookies through your web browser. Depending on your browser, you can enable or disable cookies, clear cookies or delete them after each session, and set your preferences for accessibility-related cookies.

Your Rights and Options

We respect your right to control the cookies you allow on your device. If you prefer not to have cookies used for accessibility features, you can adjust your browser settings. However, please note that blocking cookies may affect the functionality of certain accessibility features on our website.
