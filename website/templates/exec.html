Executive Board

The Interfraternity Council (IFC) at Georgia Tech is the organization that oversees the fraternities on campus. The IFC is dedicated to supporting its member fraternities by promoting leadership, teamwork, and personal growth. Through community service, leadership opportunities, and social activities, the IFC helps create a positive and inclusive experience for all fraternity members. They also manage the recruitment process and make sure that the fraternities uphold high standards of conduct and reflect Georgia Tech's values.
